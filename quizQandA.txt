Q5: The Cascades were formed by the subduction of which tectonic plate under the North American Plate?

    - Nazca Plate
    - Eurasian Plate
    - Cocos Plate
    - Indian Plate

Q6: Mt Rainier, Mt Hood, Mt St Helens, and Mt Baker were named during a 1792 expedition led by whom?
    * George Vancouver (Bristish Royal Navy Captain - led a 4 and a half year voyage around the world. His charts of the PNW coast were used by navigators for generations)
    - James Cook
    - Meriwether Lewis
    - William Clark
    - John Jacob Astor
