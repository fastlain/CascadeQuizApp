Clicking "start" button causes first question and answers to render

Selecting an answer choice will be evaluated as correct or incorrect

Incorrect or correct answer will trigger corresponding text and color changes

Correct choices will increment score

Selecting an answer will display the correct choice, explanation, img, and next button

Clicking the next button will bring up the next question

Clicking the next button on the final question will bring up the final page

The final page will display the total score, classification, and start over button

The start over button will reload the app from the beginning
